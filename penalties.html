Municipal Penalty Schedule

Penalties are assessed based on violation classification under city ordinance. Final assessment amounts may reflect administrative adjustments as permitted by law.

Violation Classification Base Assessment
Class I (Minor) $250
Class II (Moderate) $1,000
Class III (Severe) $5,000
Class IV (Critical) $25,000

Final assessment values reflect post-classification adjustments and are rounded to the nearest applicable enforcement tier.
